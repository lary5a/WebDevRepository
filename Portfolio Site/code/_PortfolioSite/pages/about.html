About

Larysa Irene is an artist based mainly in New York and London.



Born in Toronto and raised in Mainland Europe, London and Boston, she came to New York to study Integrated Digital Media at the Tandon School of Engineering within New York University.
Growing up studying fine arts she has now branched out to explore other mediums such as film, graphic design, web design and development, and other forms of media.
Though film is by far her favourite medium to work with she is excited to explore new avenues in which she can tell stories.



As a freelance writer she is excited to be spending her next semester getting back to London and taking classes where she can nurture her love for shakespeare, sonnets and dramatic literature, while also investigating what the city means to her.
